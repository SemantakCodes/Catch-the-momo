# 🥟 Catch the Momo

> "Momo is an emotion. Guff is a trap."

---

## 🌟 The Vision

Catch the Momo is a hyper-casual arcade experience built for the Nepali soul. In a world full of empty promises and "Guff," there is only one universal truth: **Momo**. Your mission is simple: Catch the juicy momos, build your legacy, and dodge the political guff falling from the sky. It's fast, it's addictive, and it's pure Nepanglish chaos.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🥟 **The Perfect Catch** | Smooth, physics-based movement using a traditional steel plate. |
| 🚫 **The Guff Filter** | Avoid golden speech bubbles like "Chito auncha" or "Paisa bholi dinchu." |
| 🌶️ **Akabare Heat** | Catch the rare chili to enter Fire Mode—everything turns red and points double! |
| 🔊 **Local Audio** | High-quality sound bites featuring iconic street calls, pressure cooker whistles, and local slang. |

---

## 🎮 How To Play

1. **Slide to Move** - Drag your finger (or mouse) to move the plate.
2. **Collect Momos** - Stack them up to increase your multiplier.
3. **Dodge the Trash** - If you catch Guff or Empty Jhol, your plate breaks and the game ends.
4. **Stay Juicy** - Catch the dipping sauce (Achar) for a temporary shield!

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

- **Engine:** Unity 6 (Rigidbody 2D / C#)
- **Physics:** Custom gravity scaling for "heavy" vs "light" falling objects
- **UI:** Modern, high-saturation "Cyber-Nepali" aesthetic
- **Platform:** Optimized for Mobile (Android/iOS) and WebGL
